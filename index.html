Lore of Appalachia

SHINING A LIGHT ON THE FOLKTALES OF APPALACHIA

The lore of Appalachia is incredibly diverse – stories based in myth, fact and mystery have been passed down for generations on stormy nights and accompanied by crackling flames. By continuing to share these stories, we are allowed to travel back in time; allowing ghosts from the past to return to the present, filling deserted buildings and barren landscapes with life, long-departed.

We invite you to join us as we unravel these well-spun tales, learning more about the past and, in turn, ourselves, along the way.
